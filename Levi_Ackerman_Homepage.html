⟢ OPERATOR PROFILE ⟢

Hi, I'm Levi Ackerman.

I work at the intersection of design and code. By day I make interfaces that hopefully don't get in people's way; by night I tinker with shaders, write about typography, and stare at very small things on screens.

I'm drawn to systems that feel both rigorous and a little dreamy — the way a good library catalog feels, or the way deep-space photography looks. This site is my attempt at that.
